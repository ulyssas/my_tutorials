1. Click Separate by Materials. This will separate the model.
2. Press tab to go to edit mode. Click third icon to enable face select mode.
3. After clicking face, you can extend selection to adjacent face from `Select Linked > Linked`.
4. Now if you press `P`, you can separate by selection.
