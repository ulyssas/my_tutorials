If the model already has MMD physics and you want to render animation in Blender, you can:

- use MMD Physics directly in Blender: click `Physics` under Model Setup > Assembly.
  This has performance issue, and it might not work properly. ([Link]({{ site.baseurl }}{% post_url 2026-06-03-009-fix-mmd-physics %}))

- use physics baking software based on MMD: MMD+[MMDBridge](https://github.com/rintrint/mmdbridge), MikuMikuMoving, NexGiMa.
  The physics will work the best, because it's MMD. You can import baked physics with VMD.

- convert physics to cloth sim with [MMD Tools Append](https://github.com/MMD-Blender/blender_mmd_tools_append):
  - [Rigid body to Cloth converter](https://youtu.be/pRJNJDFSYfk): for clothes and hair
  - [Pyramid cloth physics](https://youtu.be/Bw5LiWXHIqM): for breast physics
