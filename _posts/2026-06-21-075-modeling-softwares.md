I recommend [Metasequoia](https://www.metaseq.net/en/) for easier Blender alternative. You can make/edit MMD models as well.

Also, there are many VRM models out there, and you can easily convert them to PMX. You can make your own using [VRoid Studio](https://vroid.com/en/studio). You can even add custom clothes from Booth.
