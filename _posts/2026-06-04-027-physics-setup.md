You should setup physics in PmxEditor, because changing values is easier.

But note that you should also test physics in MMD, because physics engine works a little differently.

Adding physics is actually easy, at least compared to rigging or texturing.

Once the bones are complete, select the bones and press `Create Rigid Body/Linked Joint`.

{% include discord-gallery.html id="gallery" %}

After that, you can adjust physics to your liking!
