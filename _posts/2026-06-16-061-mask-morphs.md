1. Right click the morph and select `Vertex/UV Morph Edit`.
2. From Morph Editor window, go to Edit > `Collect Vertex - Difference`. This will hide unchanged vertices.
3. Select the part you want to remove, then from Morph Editor, click Edit > `Set Original Position`. This will reset the selected vertices to their original position.
4. And finally, click `Update` to apply the edit.
