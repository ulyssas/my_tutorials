> This only works for non-humanoid models (stages & accessories). I recommend checking out [How to convert FBX into PMX]({{ site.baseurl }}{% post_url 2026-06-03-014-fbx-to-pmx %}).

## If your model comes with Armature (human icon):

Press `Convert Model` under Model Production, select root object (axis icon) then export.

## If your model does not come with Armature (only triangle icons):

Press `Create Model` and `Attach Meshes` under Model Production, select the mesh then click `Convert to MMD` under Model Setup > Material, select root object (axis icon) then export.
