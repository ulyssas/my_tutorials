Here's some I would recommend (all free):

- SIMPLE TABS (reorder tabs in properties panel)
- Extra Objects
- fSpy (easily setup scene camera)
- Sketchfab
- VRM format
- Easy PBR (material library)
- QRemeshify (free remesher)
- Node Wrangler
- Auto Reload (reloads textures automatically)
- Robust Weight Transfer (auto weight painter)
- UniV (UV tool)

I highly recommend checking out [Awesome Blender](https://github.com/agmmnn/awesome-blender). It has a huge list of addons.
