For the head part, you should delete everything but head part.

1\. Make sure it's bone select mode (B at the top), then show hidden bones (gray circle) and double click head bone. This should select head bone and its children.

{% include discord-gallery.html id="gallery1" %}

2\. hide body part with `Refine/Masking`, then Delete/Erase Hidden Parts.

{% include discord-gallery.html id="gallery2" %}

3\. click `View Selected` in `BONE`, and click `BN-BDY` & `BDY-JNT`.

4\. then, delete hidden bones, rigidbodies and joints by inverting visibility with `R`.

{% include discord-gallery.html id="gallery3" %}

Do the same thing but inverse for the body part, then import head part.
