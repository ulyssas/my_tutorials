There are some AI tools that can auto trace videos, or even make motions from scratch.

AI based auto tracer:

- [miu's Autotrace (Google Colab)](https://qiita.com/miu200521358/items/fb0a7bcf2764d7797e26)
- [Rokoko Vision](https://www.rokoko.com/products/vision)
- [Metive](https://www.metive.app)

AI motion generator:

- [Text2Motion (by miu)](https://www.nicovideo.jp/watch/sm41278937)
- [MotionGPT](https://motion-gpt.github.io)
