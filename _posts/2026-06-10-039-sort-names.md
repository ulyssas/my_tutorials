I use [闇鍋プラグイン (EtcPlugin)](https://bowlroll.net/file/9765) in PmxEditor to export correct order as text file for later sorting.

It's called "〇〇を並べ替えるテキストファイルを作成する。" for export, and "〇〇をテキストファイルの順に並べ替えする。" for importing. It supports Rigid Bodies, bones, morphs and materials.

This window is just a launcher for plugins (called [LimitedPluginLauncher](https://bowlroll.net/file/19593))
